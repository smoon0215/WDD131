Overview

Purpose

Showcase non-professional artistry, creative projects, and personal achievements. Build a clientele for future sales.

Audience

Potiential clients. People in need of inspiration and or motivation to expand their creativity. Fellow artists and art patrons.

Dynamic elements

  1. Hero carousel - There will be a hero carousel that will rotate through 3-4 images every 5 seconds.
  2. Contact form - An html form that will send me the information via email that was inserted into the form. JS will handle form checking and submission.
  3. Filter by Year - Next to the Gallery section there is a down carrot that will be a filter by year. (this is if the contact form option does not work)

[Page 2]

Hello! I’m Samantha Moon, an artist based in Idaho and a student of Computer Science at Brigham Young University – Idaho. My artwork is an ongoing journey of exploration, where I experiment with various techniques and styles, drawing inspiration from both intuition and traditional artistic methods. While I typically work with acrylic on canvas and occasionally oils, I find myself captivated by the beauty of both nature and man-made structures. Each piece in this portfolio reflects my desire to express complex emotions and stories beyond words, capturing moments of connection and insight through visual art.
